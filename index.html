Renee Louis

I conduct research on housing and neighborhood inequality. I received my B.A. from Princeton University in 2019 and am currently a research specialist at The Eviction Lab. At the lab, I am part of the team behind the Eviction Tracking System, which monitors real-time eviction filing statistics over 26 cities in response to the current economic and public health crisis. My broader research interests include urban sociology, housing and neighborhood inequality, and the intersection of qualitative and quantitative methods in the social sciences.
